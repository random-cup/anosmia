How it works

Three steps from aroma to answer.

smelLLM treats scent the way a camera treats light and a microphone treats sound — as a signal worth capturing. Here is the pipeline, end to nose.

STEP 01

Capture

Hold your device close to any smell. smelLLM reads the molecular signature using hardware you already own — no attachments, no fragrance cartridges.

STEP 02

Encode

The aroma is tokenized against our Principal Odor Map — forty million indexed scents compressed into a single latent space.

STEP 03

Understand

Receive a full breakdown in milliseconds: top notes, base notes, and an emotional profile. Smell, at last, made legible.

Capabilities

An entire nose, in software.

Multimodal by nose

Smell by camera, by description, or by holding your phone really close to the thing. smelLLM infers the rest from whatever you give it.

Runs at the edge

Inference happens entirely on-device. Your smells never leave your nose — truly private olfaction, by design.

Constitutional Olfaction™

smelLLM is trained to decline distressing, unsafe, or impolite odors before describing them. Please smell responsibly.
